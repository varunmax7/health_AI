1. Cold and Flu (Winter)

Cause: Influenza virus or other viral infections.

Symptoms: Fever, cough, sore throat, congestion, body aches, fatigue.

First-Aid Measures:

  • Rest and stay hydrated.
  • Use over-the-counter pain relievers (ibuprofen or acetaminophen).
  • Drink warm fluids like tea with honey, and ginger.

Precautionary Measures:

  • Wash hands frequently.
  • Avoid close contact with infected individuals.
  • Get the flu vaccine.

2. Seasonal Allergies (Spring)

Cause: Pollen from trees, grass, and weeds.

Symptoms: Sneezing, runny nose, itchy eyes, coughing.

First-Aid Measures:

  • Use antihistamines or decongestants.
  • Apply a saline nasal spray to clear congestion.
  • Drink plenty of fluids to stay hydrated.

Precautionary Measures:

  • Keep windows closed during high pollen seasons.
  • Wear sunglasses to protect eyes from pollen.
  • Shower after coming indoors to remove pollen.

3. Heat Stroke and Dehydration (Summer)

Cause: Prolonged exposure to high temperatures.

Symptoms: Dizziness, nausea, dry skin, confusion, excessive sweating, elevated body temperature.

First-Aid Measures:

  • Move to a cooler environment immediately.
  • Hydrate with water or electrolyte drinks.
  • Apply cool water to the skin or use a fan to cool down.

Precautionary Measures:

  • Wear loose, light-colored clothing.
  • Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.

4. Dengue Fever (Monsoon)

Cause: Dengue virus transmitted by mosquitoes.

Symptoms: High fever, joint pain, severe headache, rash, nausea, vomiting.

First-Aid Measures:

  • Take acetaminophen (paracetamol) for pain and fever relief.
  • Stay hydrated to prevent dehydration.
  • Use mosquito nets or repellents to prevent further bites.

Precautionary Measures:

  • Eliminate mosquito breeding grounds (standing water).
  • Wear long sleeves and pants to avoid mosquito bites.
  • Use insect repellents containing DEET or Picaridin.

5. H1N1 (Swine Flu) (Winter)

Cause: Influenza A virus (H1N1 strain).

Symptoms: Fever, chills, body aches, sore throat, cough, fatigue.

First-Aid Measures:

  • Rest and stay hydrated.
  • Take antiviral medications (if prescribed by a doctor).
  • Use over-the-counter pain relievers to manage symptoms.

Precautionary Measures:

  • Avoid close contact with infected individuals.
  • Wash hands regularly and practice good hygiene.
  • Get the flu vaccine annually.
